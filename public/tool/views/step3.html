Step3/4: add indicators and gamification elements to the activity

Instruction: in this step, you can add indicators and gamification elements to the application. For example:

  • Indicators: indicators would be used to monitor students' action, meanwhile encourage students to regulate their behaviors. You can add two kinds of indicators: the group indicator or individual indicators. The group indicators would be the evaluation progress fo the group. The individual indicators would be the number of alternatives that have been checked by students.
  • Gaminifaction elements: gamification elements are used to motivate students and gain entertainment to the task. For example, a timer badge to encourage students to finish tasks within limited time; or a "comment badge" to stimulate students to submit more comments.
